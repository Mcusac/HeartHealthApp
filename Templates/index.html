Welcome to the Heart Health platform! This platform provides a method of imputting your concerns or information and receiving an answer.

The Heart Failure predictor is trained on a dataset* with many features relating to patient information and a target feature representing whether the individual will suffer from heart failure.

This platform utilizes machine learning techniques* to make a prediction on heart health. There is also an available chatbot built from utilizing a pretrained llm* and a RAG pipeline extracting data from pub med concering "Heart Health".
